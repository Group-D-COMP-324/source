Two Players Login

Connect four is a two players game, where each player login to play and tries to build their rows. This can be done horizontally, vertically or diagonally. Each player will drop in one checker piece of his color one at a time. This will give you a chance to either build your row, or stop your opponent from getting four in a row.

Single Player Login

Although this game is a two players, game, you need not have two physical players to play the game. One single player can login and play with the AI. The rules and pattern of the game are still the same as though two players logged in to play.

Gameover

The game is over when you have the first player to align four chips wins. All you have to do is connect four of your colored checker pieces in a row.
The game is also over when no player is able to align four chips and all forty-two (42) slots are filled, ending in a stalemate

Registration

All players are expected to register an account to enable them login to play this game.Registration is very easy and no cofidental information required. All you need is a username and a password.
